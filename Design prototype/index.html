What is the "Load Transport Simulation System"?


Well..... let's say that it can be one of the freshest inovations in the truck logistics allowing you to discover another side of the trucks, a more flexible and profitable one.
In this application you are the boss, you make the decisions and control everything. You can play around with your trucks, drivers and loads and see what is the best route for your deliveries! Moreover, the system will help you with estimations regarding costs, revenue, distance and duration.

Give it a try and let us know what you think !
